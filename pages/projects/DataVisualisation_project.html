QlikSense Dashboard

For Data Visualisation I created various sheets to explain the problem of wildlife strikes in aviation. On this introduction page you can view the different strikes with their damage type. As you can see lucikly there aren't that many strikes with severe damage.

Incident Occurrence & Weather Correlation

I've also created an Incident Occurrence and Weather Correlation sheet. Here, you can observe the total incidents categorized by cloud condition. Additionally, it provides insights into the months and years with the highest incident rates. These graphical representations help identify potential correlations between weather patterns and strike occurrences, facilitating a clearer understanding of the data.

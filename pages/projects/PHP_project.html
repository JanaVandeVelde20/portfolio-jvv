PHP project


In group, we developed a competition platform tailored for Thomas More.

This platform aims to streamline the organization of various competitions such as photography contests, hackathons, and more. Students have the opportunity to actively participate in these competitions. During the competition period, both teachers and students can cast their votes to determine the eventual winner."

Used technolgies: To develop this web application, we utilized PHP and the Laravel framework.

My part of the groups work

I mainly focused on developing the admin pages, which included functionalities such as managing various submission formats, competition categories, and announcement templates. Across all these pages, I implemented the CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations. Consistency in layout design was maintained throughout the entirety of these pages. To facilitate the creation process, I implemented a modal that appears when users click on the "+" icon.

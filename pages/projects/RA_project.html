Requirement Analysis Prototype

As a team, we designed prototypes for a calendar app where you can keep track of appointments, daily routines, medication,... . We began by talking with the client to understand their needs. After that, we created a use case diagram and data model. Once these were approved, we made the prototypes and showed them to the client for feedback. This helped us improve the prototypes before starting the actual development.

My part of the group work

I focused on developing various screens for the prototype, such as the calendar screens (weekly, monthly, and daily views). Additionally, I created a page for securely sharing user accounts with others. This feature enables users to share their accounts, facilitating the exchange of notes and reminders.
